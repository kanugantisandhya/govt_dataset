Important Requirements for This Dataset:

Complete & Accurate Data: Ensure all fields (License No, Validity, Category, Zone) are correctly filled to avoid missing or inconsistent records.

Standardized Formatting: Maintain uniform date formats (DD-MM-YYYY) and consistent zone names to prevent duplication or misclassification.

Updated License Validity: Regularly update expired licenses to keep track of active manufacturers.

Proper Encoding: Use UTF-8 or ISO-8859-1 encoding to avoid special character issues in company names and addresses.

Categorization of Products: Clearly define product categories to help in analysis and filtering.

Geographical Distribution Analysis: Ensure accurate zone allocation for better market insights and regulation compliance.
